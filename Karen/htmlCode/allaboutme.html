My name is Karen. I'm about to become a third grader, and I'm 8 years old. I'm a very energetic and smart person - I work out every day at dusk with my family, often my mother and sister. I've also made many webpages - I know JS, HTML, CSS, and working on SQL.

When I was 3 years old, my parent sent me and my twin sister, Kalie, to live with our grandparents in ChangZhou, China. Many of my fathers' family lived there-I had many older, younger cousins, aunts and uncles, grandaunts, granduncles, and my great-grandmother. I also went to school. My father's sister-my aunt- was a Chinese preschool teacher. Every day she would bring us to her school. All we were expected to do was sit and watch, but me and Kalie got actual knowledge out of it.

I went to preschool in America when I was four.

Life at home is simple and happy. Everyday is simillar, and quite different.

I have to say, I have a very LARGE family.
