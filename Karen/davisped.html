The Gym

The Gym is the biggest part of the school building. It is kind of like a huge basketball court, with basketball hoops on the sides. There is also two teachers: Mr. Ientile and Ms.Venezia. A curtain splits the gym into halves, one side to each teacher. Bleachers line three sides, while the fourth side is covered with mats. The gym is also for another subject, music.
