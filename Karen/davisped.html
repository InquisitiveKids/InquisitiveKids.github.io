The Gym

The Gym is the biggest part of the school building. It is kind of like a huge basketball court, with basketball hoops on the sides.
There is also two teachers: Mr. Ientile and Ms.Venezia. A curtain splits the gym into halves, one side to each teacher. Bleachers line three sides, while the fourth side is covered with mats. The gym is also for another subject, music. At assemblies and Spring Concerts, there is a projector that can roll and unroll from the ceilling.

Kids Heart Challenge

The Kids Heart Challenge is a organization and funding for kids who have heart problems. Davis School have participated in the challenge for many years.
Kids Heart Challenge for Davis School usually starts around March. There are two ways of donating money:

Donating by Form
At PE class sometime your teacher would give you a Kids Heart Challenge form for parents or guardians to fill out.
It should come with a short article, and list of prizes you could get. Remember, you do not have to donate money. It is an option. Then hand the form to your teacher.


The Kids Heart Challenge App
The next way of donating is by using the Kids Heart Challenge App. The first step after you get on the app is to register. You can get a little bracelet just by registering! Then start donating!
