Clothing
The Kimono is the traditional dressing of the Japanese. Kimonos are made from a wide variety of fibre types, including hemp, linen, silk, crepe (known as chirimen), and figured satin weaves such as rinzu, with a cross collar and wide sleeves. It is made of rectangular pieces of fabric, with a wide belt is rapped around the waist.
